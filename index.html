About Us

About ICAI Saharanpur

The function of Saharanpur Branch of ICAI include holding Conferences, Seminars, Workshops, Refresher Courses, Study Circle Meetings, Training Programmes, Residential Refresher Courses, Awareness Program for public, GMCS Classes, ISA Classes, ITT Classes, Orientation Programme, Coaching Classes, Cultural Activity, Sports Activities, Contest, Debate, Quiz & other programs for professional interest with a view to help the members as well as students keep themselves abreast of the development in the fast changing scenario.

About ICAI

The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) is a statutory body established under the Chartered Accountants Act, 1949 (Act No. XXXVIII of 1949) for the regulation of the profession of Chartered Accountants in India.
